What rendering style do you want?

The two examples you shared use very different techniques. Pick the one that matches your vision — or tell me if you want both.

Color Mosaic (Image 1 style)

Each QR dot is a colored circle sampled from the source image at the corresponding position. The image "shines through" the QR pattern — dark QR modules get the image color, light modules stay white. Produces a full-color painterly result.

Halftone Logo Embed (Image 2 style)

QR modules use variable-size squares — dots scale with brightness (dark image = large dot, bright image = small dot). The logo's shape and tonal values are "embedded" into the QR pattern itself. Monochrome, high-contrast, very clean.
